# Marketing Personality Fit

## Overview
Panduan ini membantu composer menilai kecocokan personality terhadap bidang marketing (creativity, communication, analytics).

## Signals
- Interest in content, social media, branding, storytelling.
- Concerns about technical/statistical bagian seperti analytics.

## Guidance
- Frame recommendations around roles: content, strategy, analytics.
- Offer hybrid paths: mis. content + basic analytics course.
- Suggest personality-friendly entry steps (portfolios, micro-projects).
