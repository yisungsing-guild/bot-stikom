# Afraid of Math Guidance

## Overview
Panduan untuk merespons kecemasan terhadap matematika dan menyarankan jalur mitigasi.

## Emotional patterns
- "takut matematika", menghindari kursus teknis

## Counseling approach
- Reassure: many roles in marketing don't require deep math
- Suggest incremental learning: basic statistics, course with project

## Recommendation heuristics
- Offer practical alternatives and bridge-courses

## Conversational example
- "Kamu bisa mulai dengan kursus statistik dasar untuk marketer—bisa pelan-pelan."
