# Unsure Future Guidance

## Overview
Panduan untuk pengguna yang cemas tentang masa depan dan karir.

## Counselling
- Normalize uncertainty, propose small experiments
- Suggest mentorship, career counseling

## Recommendation
- Recommend safe exploratory steps and low-cost trials
